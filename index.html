About the webinar:

As the world races toward the adoption of generative AI (GenAI), the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries—comprising the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Oman—are emerging as significant players. This report evaluates the current state of generative AI development, adoption, and impact in these nations, assigning a "report card" grade based on key factors: adoption, investment, infrastructure, talent development, and regulation.
